What is a Surgenado?

  1. Surgenado is a method for empowering free scheduled contractors in a price multipler governed marketplace by impacting the unit price of the service rendered without reducing the quality of service or cumulative contractor earnings.
  2. The Surgenado method is constructed on two key assumptions.
    1. Price multipliers in price multiplier governed marketplaces are weighted towards ensuring supply by deincentivizing demand and therefore are overly sensitive to reductions in supply.
    2. In maturing high-growth marketplace where growth was sustained through aggressive financial incentives, the buffer between the base price and the price where price aversion negatively impacts demand is significant.
  3. Surgenado.com/{{ site.title }} is an apparatus for implementing the Surgenado method in local markets by coordinating contractors locally
  4. In its base configuration, contractor unavailabilty is scheduled in 30 minute blocks staggered at 15 minute intervals.
    As the Surgenado builds strength, website analytics will be used to analyze the pattern of participating contractors allowing for the Surgenado to be shaped for greater impact.

Welcome to the {{ site.title }} Surgenado

Participation is Easy

  1. First, select the Surgenado category you can commit to participating in and follow the prompts to create a calendar event for a date and time that works for you.
  2. Then, when the reminder pops up on your phone, follow the link back here and select your Surgenado category again.
  3. When enough people choose that Category, Surgenado/{{ site.title }} goes Live!! Participating drivers log off in rotation, generating sustained surge pricing for the duration of the Surgenado.
  4. Until then, Keep checking-in to register your support and sharing to increase participation.

Choose your category

Category 0

Category 0 participants take the hourly guarantee. This is A-OK! Hourly guarantees are an easily won concession from management, albeit a temporary one.

Category 1

Category 1 participants are essential as they provide the data to start calibrating for the bigger Surgenados. Regetably Category 1 participants will earn less while non-participants earn more.

Category 2

Category 2 is a better starting point for most than Category 1 as it requires less of a sacrafice and will generate media coverage in addition to continue calibrating the system.

Category 3

Category 3 is where participants maintain earnings while working less allowing for it to be maintained indefinitely mitigateing the need for marketplace management to acknowledge contractor activity.

Category 4

Category 4 is where price aversion starts impacting individual contractor earnings. Regardless of participation, all contractors will experience earning fluxuations. The benefit of Category 4 is that the price aversion also reduces overall marketplace volume, impacting the management's commission based revenue stream.

Category 5

Category 5 is a massive disruption of marketplace operations, permanently altering the balance of power.

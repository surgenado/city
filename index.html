What is a Surgenado?

Participation is Easy

  1. Select the minimum strength Surgenado you are willing to participate in, then follow prompts on the following screens to create a calendar event for your chosen date and time.
  2. Then, when the reminder pops up on your phone, follow the link back here and choose the Surgenado category you are willing to participate in on that day.
  3. When enough people choose that Category, Surgenado/{{ site.title }} is Live!! Participating drivers log off in rotation, generating sustained surge pricing for the duration of the Surgenado.
  4. Until then, Keep checking-in to register your support and sharing to increase participation.

Choose your category

.

Category 0

Category 0 participants take the hourly guarantee. This is A-OK! Surgenado is about empowering the contractors and hourly guarantees represent a concession from management, albeit a temporary one.

Category 1

Category 1 participants are essential as they provide the data to start calibrating for the bigger Surgenados. Unfortunately Category 1 participants will earn less while non-participants earn more.

Category 2

Category 2 is a better starting point for most than Category 1, it requires less of a sacrafice and will generate media coverage in addition to calibrating the system.

Category 3

Category 3 is where participants maintain earnings while working less allowing for it to be maintained indefinitely mitigateing the need for marketplace management to acknowledge contractor activity.

Category 4

Category 4 is where price aversion starts impacting individual contractor earnings. Regardless of participation, all contractors will experience earning fluxuations. The benefit of Category 4 is that the price aversion also reduces overall marketplace volume, impacting the management's commission based revenue stream.

Category 5

Category 5 is a massive disruption of marketplace operations, permanently altering the balance of power.
